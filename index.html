Who we are

We are a five-person team with a great deal of experience in our respective fields. "Speed Unleashed, Power Unmatched" is our motto, and everything we do embodies its brilliance. Perseverance, passion, and excellence are among our key values. Together, we are sure that we can accomplish our objectives in a methodical and successful way. Together, we actively work to improve society by creating an atmosphere that is conducive to embracing and participating in STEM activities. Our team members come from diverse backgrounds, bringing a wide range of skills and knowledge to the table. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in our fields and are always striving to innovate and push the limits. By fostering a culture of collaboration and creativity, we believe that we can make a real difference in the world and inspire others to do the same. Our commitment to excellence and our passion for what we do drive us to achieve our goals and make a positive impact on society.

THE PROTOTYPES

Viper

This is our first prototype which we came up with after lots of researching and trial and errors. The name 'Viper' comes from the car Dodge Viper, sharing close resemblance to our car. It's main feature and center of attention was the tall car body extending towards the back, comprising of the back wings only. The dive to the front nose was very steep, thus providing an unstable air flow around the car. The side skirts attached to the side body of the car provided some directional air flow, but the net flow was quite off the expectations and not very good to use in this big competition.
